Bro Culture

It is quite common for startups to adopt "cool bro culture" to try to appeal to developers. They offer beer on tap, have bean bags, and decorate the space with nerd references. In an effort to attract talent, they are actually deterring women from the field.

Non-Stereotypical Environment

Getting rid of bro culture doesn't reduce men's interest in pursuing computer science, but it does increase women's interest. Also, it isn't enough to get rid of stereotypical culture, it needs to be disconfirmed. By replacing star wars posters with, for example, a landscape photo, it disconfirms beliefs of startups being for nerdy men.
